Day 1

Grilled Chicken Ole 

Ingredients 

4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 

Marinade (recipe below) 

Juice of 1 fresh lime 

3 medium tomatoes, diced 

. cup red onion, chopped 

1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced 

. cup yellow pepper, diced 

. cup fresh cilantro, chopped 

4 cups fresh broccoli (or frozen, thawed) 

8 cups fresh spinach 

2 cloves garlic, minced Marinade Ingredients juice of 1 fresh lime 

dash of cayenne Pepper 

. cup fresh cilantro, chopped 

Directions: 

o Mix marinade ingredients in Ziploc bag 

o Add chicken and refrigerate for 30 minutes 

o Mix lime juice, tomatoes, onion, jalapeno, yellow pepper, and cilantro in small bowl and set salsa aside 

o Remove chicken from marinade and cook on grill pan for 6-10 minutes or until juices run clear 

o Remove from grill and set aside 

o Grill broccoli spears on grill pan for 8-10 minutes, turning frequently 

o Add spinach leaves and garlic to a large skillet spritzed with Extra Virgin Olive Oil over medium heat 

o Saut? spinach until just wilted 

o Divide the wilted spinach onto four plates. Top spinach with chicken breast and add a scoop of salsa 

o Serve broccoli spears on side 

o Serves 4
